100% Free to Attend

No Barriers. No Fees. Just Community.

We believe the best business connections shouldn't come with a price tag. Darlington First events are completely free to attend — one of very few free networking events across the entire North East region.

By removing the barriers to entry, we're opening the door for young entrepreneurs just getting started and experienced business leaders alike. Everyone deserves a seat at the table.

Our networking events will vary — some traditional networking, others more creative: think watching local football or rugby together, a game of padel or tennis, bowling, and plenty more. The one thing we're committed to is a free, no-hassle monthly coffee morning — with venues rotating each month to support as many local Darlington businesses as possible.

Just a heads-up: While our events are free, we do ask that everyone pays for their own coffee. It's our little way of supporting the brilliant local venues who host us.

Why Darlington?

The gateway to the North East

Darlington is the gateway to the North East — connecting Durham, Newcastle, and every town in between. With major government investment in the train station, Teesside International Airport, and the relocation of HM Treasury, Darlington has never been better positioned for business.

Major Transport Hub

Darlington station sits on the East Coast Main Line — connecting London, Edinburgh, and every major North East city.

HM Treasury Relocation

Major government investment is transforming Darlington into one of the most strategically important business locations in the UK.

Teesside Airport

Teesside International Airport connects the region nationally and internationally, making Darlington a true gateway city.

About Darlington First

We're more than a networking group. We're a movement to put Darlington on the map as the North East's premier business destination.

Who We Are

Darlington First was born out of a simple belief: that Darlington deserves a world-class business community. We're a collective of local business leaders, entrepreneurs, and community champions who want to see our town thrive.

We run relaxed, informal networking events — completely free to attend — because we believe the best connections shouldn't come with a price tag. From monthly coffee mornings to creative events like watching local sport together or a game of padel, no two events are the same.

100% Free to Attend — no membership fees, no hidden costs, no long-term commitments.

Darlington First is part of the First Connections Group — a wider ecosystem of networking, awards, and podcasting that champions businesses across the North East.

Our Values

What drives us

Community First

We put the people of Darlington and the North East at the heart of everything we do. Real relationships over transactions.

No Barriers

Everyone deserves a seat at the table. We remove financial and social barriers so anyone can connect and grow.

Real Social Value

We're not just about business cards. We generate genuine social value — inspiring stories, practical support, and lasting impact.

Growth for All

From first-time founders to seasoned CEOs — we bridge the gap between ambition and experience so everyone grows together.

Our Story

How Darlington First came to be.

The Idea Takes Root

Frustrated by the lack of free, accessible networking in the North East, the founders of First Connections set out to create something different — a space where everyone belongs.

First Coffee Morning

The inaugural Darlington First coffee morning brought together local business leaders in a relaxed setting, proving that great connections don't need a price tag.

High Street Heroes Launches

We introduced the North East High Street Heroes Awards — celebrating the unsung businesses making a real difference on our high streets.

Regional Podcast Goes Live

The First Connections podcast launched, spotlighting the stories, insights, and voices driving business forward across the North East.

Growing Across the Region

With sponsors on board and a growing community, Darlington First is expanding — becoming the first place businesses come to connect in the North East.
